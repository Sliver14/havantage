Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our training programs, services, and how we can help you succeed.

1. Is the training beginner-friendly?

Yes. You don't need prior tech experience. We guide you through each step.

2. Are sessions online or in person?

All sessions are online and live. You'll join interactive classes and real-world projects.

3. How long is the training?

Each program runs for 10 weeks.

4. What tools will I learn in QA or PM training?

You'll use tools like JIRA, TestRail, Postman, Trello, and more — all real-world.

5. Do you help with internships or job placement?

Yes. Top participants are connected to internships and job opportunities through our network.

6. Can we outsource our QA or PM work to Havantage?

Absolutely. We provide both short-term and ongoing support for companies.

7. How are you different from other bootcamps?

We focus on real experience, not just content. You'll work on real projects, receive mentorship, and build true confidence.

8. Do you offer corporate training?

Yes. We customize training for companies based on skill gaps, tools, and delivery goals.

9. Do you offer custom advisory for teams?

Yes. From process setup to leadership coaching, we support your team at every level.

10. How do I get started?

Use the contact form or apply to the training cohort. We'll respond quickly.
