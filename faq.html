FAQ

Common questions about Havantage.

Answers about programs, payment, services, talent placement, and forms.

How do I join a program?

Choose a program, fill the central program interest form, make payment by transfer, and send your payment evidence. Havantage shares program access details after payment confirmation.

Where can I see the program fee?

Each individual program page shows its current fee and class joining instructions.

How do I pay?

Payment is by bank transfer. The bank details are displayed during the joining process and after form submission. The current build contains placeholders that must be replaced with Havantage official account details before publishing.

What happens after I fill the form?

After you submit the form, you will see the next steps for payment and follow-up. You may also join the general Havantage community for updates while your enrollment is being processed.

Can organisations request custom training?

Yes. Organisations can request corporate training in leadership, AI readiness, productivity, strategic thinking, and professional effectiveness.

Do you provide organisational support services?

Yes. Havantage supports organisations with PMO setup, delivery governance, software testing, quality assurance, corporate solutions, and team training programs.

How does talent placement work?

Professionals can join the talent network, and organisations can request trained or pre-vetted talent. Havantage reviews submissions and follows up.
